Why Austin?

Keepin' it weird since 2000.

This city is known for its vibrant city life and strange encounters. Austin is a great place to explore history, see live music, and enjoy Tex-Mex and BBQ.

My Favorite Restaurants

The Oasis

Here you can experience picturesque views and an embracing breeze. The Oasis offers live music and a romantic aura.

Address:

6550 Comanche Trail, Austin, TX, 78732

What I love

The views are absolutely breathtaking. This location provides a serene dining experience, and excellent food. Check out the website for their live shows.

Visit Site

Chuy's

It all begain in the 80's with a shack and a dream. Chuy's is bringing Tex-Mex to Austinites with a funky feel. they're known for an eclectic style and delicious margs.

Address:

1728 Barton Springs Rd, Austin, TX, 78704

What I love

This is a great place for insta worthy photos. As an OG Tex-Mex location, Chuy's honors Elvis Presley in some pretty electric ways. Check it out!

Visit Site

VooDoo Donuts

These donuts are absolutely incredible! If you're looking for something that screams "Keep Ausitn Weird" then come check out this donut shop.

Address:

212 E 6th Street, Austin, TX, 78701

What I love

This place has original flavors like maple bacon and fruit loop donuts. If you want to wow your taste buds, go check it out!
